About Arborist Newcastle

Arborist Newcastle was founded with a simple mission: to provide exceptional tree care services to the Newcastle and Brisbane communities. With over a decade of experience in professional arboriculture, our team has built a reputation for delivering safe, reliable, and high-quality tree services. We started as a small operation driven by a passion for trees and a commitment to safety, and have grown into one of the region's most trusted tree service providers. Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our tree services

What areas do you service?

We provide professional tree lopping and removal services throughout Newcastle and Brisbane. Our service area covers residential and commercial properties within a reasonable distance from our base. We're equipped to handle both routine maintenance and emergency tree services. For specific location inquiries, please contact us directly at quote@arboristnewcastle.com.au or call 0439455003 to confirm service availability for your area.

How much does tree removal cost?

Tree removal costs vary depending on several factors including tree size, location, complexity, and accessibility. Rather than providing generic pricing, we offer free on-site quotes tailored to your specific situation. We believe in transparent pricing with no hidden fees. Once we assess your property, we'll provide a detailed quote that breaks down all costs. Contact us to schedule your free consultation and receive an accurate estimate for your tree removal needs.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes, absolutely. All our arborists are fully licensed and certified professionals with proper credentials. We maintain comprehensive insurance coverage for all our work, including public liability and property damage insurance. This means you can have complete peace of mind knowing that every tree service is performed by qualified professionals and your property is fully protected. We're happy to provide proof of our licenses and insurance upon request.

How quickly can you respond to emergency requests?

We pride ourselves on fast response times for emergency tree situations. Many emergency requests receive same-day service, depending on the time of call and current workload. Storm damage, fallen trees, and hazardous branches are treated with priority. Call us immediately at 0439455003 if you have an emergency tree situation, and we'll do our best to assist you quickly. Our emergency response team is equipped to handle urgent tree removal and safety concerns promptly and professionally.

What happens to the tree debris after removal?

We handle all debris removal professionally and responsibly. Wood materials are often chipped for mulch or recycled where possible. Larger logs can be cut to size for firewood if desired. We ensure your property is left clean and tidy after every job. All waste is disposed of in an environmentally responsible manner in compliance with local regulations. We offer various debris removal options, and we'll discuss the best solution for your specific situation during your consultation.
